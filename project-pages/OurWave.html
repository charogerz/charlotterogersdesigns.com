Our Wave Newsletter Redesign

A series of emails constructed for and sent out on behalf of the nonprofit organization Our Wave
Project Details
I have volunteered at Our Wave since April 2023, helping them completely restructure and redesign their newsletter system. Previously, they had been sending out infrequent and long bulleted list emails for all updates. I have since been the lead on writing and sending these emails out multiple times a month, designing templates first in Figma with their lead Desginer before templating and formatting them in the platform Sendgrid. I have mapped out and helped them to create newsletters and subscriber groups focusing on introductions to organizational partners, donation inquiries, monthly blog updates, upcoming events, and impact reports.
