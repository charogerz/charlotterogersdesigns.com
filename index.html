About Me
I am a second year graduate student at Parsons School of Design in NYC studying Design and Technology. I graduated in 2022 from NC State University with a B.S. in Graphic Communications and a minor in Social Work. My designs intentionally use the Design Justice framework to aim for the intersection of beauty and function and to meet the needs and desires of my audience. This is where true accessibility is found. No work is perfect but I am proud of what I have created and the projects I have supported and completed. I also look forward to the future and what I am able to accomplish with more expereience and learning opportunities.
