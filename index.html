About Me
I am a 2D designer who loves creative coding. I hold a B.S. in Graphic Communications from North Carolina State University and a M.F.A. in Design and Technology from Parsons School of Design. My designs intentionally use the Design Justice framework to aim for the intersection of beauty and function and to meet the needs and desires of my audience. This is where true accessibility is found. No design work is ever truly perfect but I am proud of what I have created and the projects I have supported and completed. I always look forward to the future and what I am able to accomplish with more experience and learning opportunities.
