Musical Mockup

Designing materials for a musician's fictitious album and tour for a Typography course
Project Details
I was tasked with designing and mocking-up a fictitious record label, cover, and tour poster for a musical artist of my choosing for my Typography course in the spring of 2024. I chose Laufey as an artist I am fond of and created a concept that I felt fit her genre and style as an artist. I chose to highlight the title of this album with the Monotype typeface "Tw Cen MT", complementing it with the artist's name in the "Freestyle Script" typeface to give it some decorative edge. The final products, after class critiques and edits, coordinate in color and type without matching too closely to be given some depth and become part of a series. I used Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for this project.
