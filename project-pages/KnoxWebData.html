Logo for Knox Web & Data
Logo designed for a small business based in Knoxville, Tennessee
Project Details
I worked with client Knox Web & Data on a logo for their website and potential printed documents. They requested this logo be related to Tennessee and have some digital feel to it. After a few mock-ups and iterations, we came up with something that fit this description and became the focus of their brand. They have this logo featured in both black and white in different places.
You can see the logo active and dynamic on their website HERE.
